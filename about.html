About Next Gen

Developing professionals through education, collaboration, friendly competition, and meaningful community impact.

Who We Are

Next Gen is a nonprofit business league dedicated to helping insurance professionals grow through education, collaboration, leadership development, and meaningful accountability. We bring together agency owners and agency team members who are committed to continuously improving their knowledge, strengthening their skills, and making a lasting impact within the communities they serve.

Our Mission

Next Gen exists to cultivate a community of professionals committed to continuous learning, collaboration, and service. Through education, mentorship, and friendly competition, we empower members to strengthen their knowledge, develop professionally, and better serve the individuals, families, and communities that place their trust in them.

How Next Gen Works

Monthly Learning Sessions

Each month, Next Gen hosts educational meetings both virtually and in person where members share ideas, proven strategies, leadership lessons, and practical knowledge that can immediately be applied within their own agencies.

Collaboration Through Shared Success

Members openly share best practices, successful systems, and real-world experiences so everyone has the opportunity to learn and improve together.

Friendly Competition

Next Gen encourages healthy competition among participating members and agencies through monthly performance recognition, Production Clubs, and member spotlights. Friendly competition creates accountability, celebrates achievement, and motivates everyone to continually raise the standard while learning from one another's success.

Serving Our Communities

Everything we do ultimately points back to the people and communities we serve. By becoming more knowledgeable, more prepared, and stronger professionals, our members are better equipped to provide exceptional service, build lasting relationships, and make a meaningful difference for the families and businesses that rely on them every day.

Recognizing Excellence

Next Gen proudly recognizes members who demonstrate professional growth, leadership, integrity, service, and dedication through Team Member Spotlights, Production Clubs, and annual recognition programs.

Our Commitment

Next Gen is committed to building a culture where learning never stops, success is shared, and excellence is pursued together. Through education, collaboration, friendly competition, and a shared commitment to serving others, we strive to develop professionals who continually raise the standard for themselves, their agencies, and the communities they proudly serve.
