Next Gen is a nonprofit business league dedicated to helping insurance professionals grow through education, collaboration, leadership development, and meaningful community impact.

Who We Are

Next Gen brings together agency owners and agency team members who believe that professional growth is strongest when knowledge is shared, success is celebrated, and individuals are encouraged to continually improve.

Through monthly educational programming, networking opportunities, leadership development, and collaborative learning, members strengthen their professional skills while building lasting relationships across participating agencies.

Our Mission

Next Gen exists to cultivate a community of professionals committed to continuous learning, collaboration, accountability, and service. Through education, mentorship, and friendly competition, we empower members to strengthen their knowledge, grow professionally, and better serve the individuals, families, businesses, and communities that place their trust in them.

Monthly Learning Sessions

Every month Next Gen hosts educational meetings both virtually and in person where members exchange ideas, discuss successful strategies, and learn practical skills that can immediately strengthen their agencies.

Collaboration Through Shared Success

Members openly share best practices, office systems, leadership ideas, and real-world experiences so everyone has the opportunity to learn, improve, and grow together.

Continuous Growth

Growth is at the heart of everything we do. Through consistent learning, mentorship, performance tracking, and shared experiences, members are encouraged to challenge themselves, celebrate progress, and continually develop both personally and professionally.

Serving Our Communities

Everything we do points back to the communities we serve. By investing in the growth of our members, we help professionals become better prepared to provide exceptional service, build lasting relationships, and positively impact the people who rely on them every day.

Recognizing Excellence

Next Gen celebrates the accomplishments of its members through Production Clubs, Team Member Spotlights, and annual recognition programs that highlight leadership, professionalism, integrity, collaboration, growth, and service.

Our Commitment

We are committed to building a culture where learning never stops, success is shared, and excellence is pursued together. Through education, collaboration, and friendly competition, we strive to develop professionals who continually raise the standard for themselves, their agencies, and the communities they proudly serve.

Whether you're looking to strengthen your leadership, learn from experienced professionals, or become part of a community committed to excellence, we'd love to connect.
